Welcome to Tokyo Draft!

Tokyo Draft is a Two Team Game.

Each Team begins with a pool of NBA Players in the middle

Each Team Selects a group of 5 Players

Players are selected in a Draft format.

Beginning with Team A, picks are traded back and forth until each Team is filled

After each Team is filled, the Game is played and a Winner is determined!
